A Guide for Busy Dads

The 7-Day
Dad Bod Reset

Kickstart

A simple plan for busy dads who want to get back in shape — without the gym, without complicated diets, without the BS.

By Arif [Placeholder]

Why This Exists

I'm 41. I'm a dad. And I'm in the best shape of my life.

Not because I have some secret — but because I stopped overcomplicating it. I stopped chasing the latest trend, stopped buying supplements I didn't need, stopped thinking I needed two hours in the gym to see results.

This 7-day plan is exactly how I'd start if I was starting over. Real food. 30 minutes a day. No BS.

If you follow it properly, you'll feel different by Day 4. By Day 7, you'll wonder why you ever made it harder than this.

How to Use This Guide
1

Follow each day as written. Don't skip ahead, don't modify. Trust the process for 7 days — then decide.

2

30 minutes for the workout. That's it. No more. You're proving to yourself that consistency beats marathon sessions.

3

Track everything on the habit tracker (last page). Consistency beats intensity. Every single time.

Section 01

Meal Plans

Day 1 — Keep It Simple

Breakfast

3 eggs scrambled, 2 slices sourdough toast, handful of spinach

Lunch

Chicken breast, white rice, steamed broccoli. Season however you want.

Dinner

Salmon fillet, sweet potato, green beans with garlic

Snack

Apple + handful of almonds

Day 2 — Build the Habit

Breakfast

Greek yoghurt, handful of blueberries, drizzle of honey, 2 tablespoons oats

Lunch

Turkey mince lettuce wraps with diced tomato, cucumber, squeeze of lemon

Dinner

Lean beef stir-fry with peppers, onions, soy sauce, served over rice

Snack

2 boiled eggs, pinch of salt and pepper

Day 3 — Find Your Rhythm

Breakfast

2-egg omelette with mushrooms, tomatoes, and a slice of sourdough

Lunch

Tinned tuna mixed with olive oil, lemon, red onion. Served on 2 rice cakes.

Dinner

Baked chicken thighs with roasted potatoes, carrots, and a side of peas

Snack

Banana + tablespoon of peanut butter

Day 4 — Recovery Day

Breakfast

Porridge oats with sliced banana, cinnamon, and a drizzle of honey

Lunch

Leftover chicken thighs from yesterday, sliced on a mixed salad with olive oil dressing

Dinner

Prawn stir-fry with egg noodles, pak choi, garlic, soy sauce

Snack

Carrot sticks + hummus

Day 5 — Push Through

Breakfast

3 scrambled eggs with smoked salmon on 1 slice of rye toast

Lunch

Chicken wrap — grilled chicken, lettuce, tomato, light mayo in a wholemeal tortilla

Dinner

Lamb mince kofta with rice, cucumber-yoghurt dip, and a simple side salad

Snack

Small handful of mixed nuts + a pear

Day 6 — Almost There

Breakfast

Overnight oats — oats, yoghurt, milk, chia seeds, berries. Made the night before.

Lunch

Tuna steak (or tinned tuna), quinoa or rice, roasted courgette and peppers

Dinner

Homemade beef burger (no bun) with sweet potato wedges and coleslaw

Snack

Greek yoghurt + handful of walnuts

Day 7 — Finish Strong

Breakfast

Avocado on sourdough with 2 poached eggs, chilli flakes, squeeze of lemon

Lunch

Grilled chicken salad — mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, feta, olive oil dressing

Dinner

Pan-fried sea bass, roasted Mediterranean veg, couscous

Snack

Dark chocolate (2 squares) + a handful of strawberries

Section 02

Workouts

30 minutes. No equipment. No gym. Rest 60-90 seconds between sets unless stated otherwise.

Day 1 — Full Body Foundation
Push-ups 3 x 15
Bodyweight squats 3 x 20
Plank hold 3 x 30 sec
Reverse lunges (each leg) 3 x 12
Mountain climbers 3 x 20
Day 2 — Upper Body Focus
Diamond push-ups 3 x 12
Pike push-ups 3 x 10
Tricep dips (chair or step) 3 x 15
Superman holds 3 x 20 sec
Plank shoulder taps 3 x 16
Day 3 — Lower Body Focus
Jump squats 3 x 15
Walking lunges (each leg) 3 x 12
Glute bridges 3 x 20
Wall sit 3 x 40 sec
Calf raises 3 x 25
Day 4 — Active Recovery

No structured workout today. Your body needs to recover.

Go for a 30-minute walk. Outside. No treadmill.

Stretch for 10 minutes when you get back — hamstrings, hip flexors, shoulders, chest.

Hydrate properly. Eat on plan. Sleep well tonight.

Day 5 — Full Body Strength
Wide push-ups 4 x 15
Bulgarian split squats (each leg) 3 x 10
Plank to push-up 3 x 10
Single-leg glute bridges (each leg) 3 x 12
Dead bugs 3 x 16
Day 6 — HIIT Circuit

4 rounds. 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest per exercise. 1 minute rest between rounds.

Burpees 40 sec
High knees 40 sec
Jump squats 40 sec
Push-ups 40 sec
Plank hold 40 sec
Day 7 — Core + Mobility
Bicycle crunches 3 x 20
Side plank (each side) 3 x 30 sec
Leg raises 3 x 15
World's greatest stretch (each side) 3 x 8
Cat-cow stretches 3 x 10
