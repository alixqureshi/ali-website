Stupid Simple

How to actually film

No tripod. No ring light. No editing suite. Here are the only 4 steps.

1

Set Up

10 seconds

Prop your phone against something. Kitchen counter, water bottle, window ledge. Natural light. That's your studio.

2

Hook First

3 seconds

Start talking BEFORE you think you're ready. The first thing out of your mouth should make someone stop scrolling. "Dads, stop eating this." / "This is why you're still fat." / "I'm 41 and here's what I eat."

3

Deliver

15-25 seconds

One point. Not five. One thing you want them to know. Keep it under 30 seconds total.

4

Post

2 minutes

Open CapCut. Import. Auto-captions. Pick a bold caption style. Export. Post to Instagram Reels. Cross-post to TikTok. Done.

Zero Pressure

Nobody has to see your first attempts

What are Trial Reels?

Instagram lets you post reels that only go to non-followers. Your existing followers — your friends, family, colleagues — never see them. It's a testing ground with zero social pressure.

Your first 2 weeks

Record 20 trial reels in your first 2 weeks. Test different formats. See what gets views. See what feels natural. Then double down on your winning format.

The point isn't to go viral. The point is to find YOUR format — the one that feels easy and authentic to you.
