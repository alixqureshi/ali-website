The Truth About Audience

You don't need 100K followers

Most people think they need a massive audience before they can make money. That's the influencer model. This isn't that. This is the intentional audience model — where 500 of the right followers is worth more than 50,000 passive ones.

The Math

£749

per enrollment

14

new members/month for £10K

3–4

conversions per week

500

followers is enough

If you have 500 followers and 10% engage in your DMs, that's 50 conversations per month. Converting 3–4 out of 50 is a 6–8% close rate — very conservative when you're selling to people who already trust you.

Lead Efficiency > Lead Volume

50 dads who watch every reel, reply to stories, and DM you questions are worth more than 50,000 people who scrolled past once. The goal isn't reach — it's conversations.

Chat-to-Close

You're not posting a link and hoping people buy. At £749, the sale happens in a DM conversation. A voice note from Arif. A real chat. That's how trust-based selling works — and it works with tiny audiences.

The 7-Hour Rule

Research shows people need ~7 hours of content consumption before buying from a creator. One YouTube video per week + daily reels gets a follower to 7 hours within a month. You don't need millions of views — you need the same 500 people watching consistently.

The Flywheel Compounds

Every member who gets results becomes a testimonial. Every testimonial becomes content. Every piece of content attracts more of the right dads. You don't need to go viral — you need a flywheel that compounds every month.

Content

You don't need to be an influencer

I know filming content feels weird. You're not a content creator. You don't want to dance on TikTok or do transitions. Good news: that's not what works in this niche. The content that performs best is raw, real, and simple — a dad talking to his phone about food and fitness. That's it.

Why It's Easier Than You Think

You're not creating content. You're sharing what you already do.

Filming your meal, your workout, your walk with the kids. You're already doing this stuff daily.

No editing needed at the start.

CapCut has auto-captions. Film, add captions, post. Under 5 minutes.

Nobody watches production value in fitness.

The biggest fitness creators (Eddie Abbew, etc.) film on their phone in a supermarket. Raw wins.

Trial reels remove the pressure.

Instagram lets you post trial reels that your followers don't see. It's a testing ground. Nobody judges you because nobody sees it until you decide to publish.
