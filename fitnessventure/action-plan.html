Phase 1 — Blockers

Do these first

These block the landing page from going live. Nothing launches until these exist.

1

Film your 2-minute video (VSL)

30 minutes • Unblocks the landing page • Film horizontal (landscape)

A raw, talking-to-camera video for the landing page. This is the single most important piece of content for the entire brand. No script. No editing. Just you, talking to a dad who's watching on his phone.

Story beats (in order)

  1. 1. "I've trained my whole life."
  2. 2. "Then I became a dad with a full-time job."
  3. 3. "Everything that used to work stopped working — I couldn't get to the gym, couldn't spend 90 minutes, couldn't eat separately from my family."
  4. 4. "So I had to figure it out from scratch. 30 minutes. At home. Real food my family actually eats."
  5. 5. "Now I'm 41 and in the best shape of my life. Not despite being a dad. As a dad."
  6. 6. "I built this program so you don't have to figure it out alone. I coach you 1-1 — your training, your nutrition, your recovery."
  7. 7. "If you're a dad who's done starting over, apply below."

Rules

  • • Do NOT say "I've been where you are" — you haven't been overweight. Say "I fight the same battle you do."
  • • Do NOT read from a script. Talk like you're explaining this to a mate at the school gates.
  • • Film HORIZONTAL (landscape, 16:9). This goes on the landing page, not Instagram.
  • • Film at home — living room, garden. NOT a gym. The setting IS the message.
  • • Phone camera. Natural light. No ring light.
  • • Film 3 takes. Send all 3 to Ali. Don't overthink it.

Filming rules

  • • Vertical (9:16). All Reels are vertical. Only the VSL is horizontal.
  • • Raw > polished. Film on your phone. No transitions needed.
  • • Hook in the first 1-2 seconds. Start mid-sentence or with a confrontational statement.
  • • Under 60 seconds. 30-45 seconds is ideal.
  • • End with: "Link in bio" or "DM me RESET"
  • • Film in real settings: kitchen, living room, park. NOT a gym.
6

7-Day Challenge content (lead magnet)

1-2 hours to write • +1 hour if filming video intros

The free 7-day email challenge captures emails. You provide the workout and food swap content for each day.

  • ☐ 7 workouts (30 min each, home-friendly, no equipment, beginner-suitable)
  • ☐ 7 food swaps (one per day, family-friendly, 1-2 sentences each)
  • ☐ Optional: 30-60 sec video intro per day ("Day 3. Your legs are going to hate me.")
