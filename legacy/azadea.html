02 / The Split That Matters

AI works for clothes. It doesn't work for food.

Fashion Brands

Jeans, suits, outfits — these are great products to show. The product itself and the brand story translate well to AI-generated statics, motion graphics, and AI video. We can produce at volume and test aggressively.

Salsa Jeans, Boggi Milano, Punt Roma

AI statics + motion + AI video — full mix

F&B Brands

AI can't create realistic food imagery, and it can't show someone eating — it just looks fake. We need real photos and real footage as the foundation. From there, AI handles the ad design: static ads, motion graphics, layouts, offer framing. But the food itself has to be real.
